Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes! You can cancel your subscription anytime from your profile page. There are no cancellation fees, and you'll continue to enjoy benefits until the end of your current billing period.
How does the monthly product budget work?
Your monthly budget is a spending allowance at discounted rates. For example, on Spice Pro you can purchase up to ₹1,200 worth of products per month with your 10% membership discount applied automatically.
When will I receive my surprise box?
Elite members receive their curated surprise box within the first 5 business days of each month. Each box contains handpicked premium spices, teas, and exclusive blends worth ₹500+.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Absolutely! You can upgrade anytime and the new plan takes effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and wallets. Your subscription will auto-renew monthly or annually based on your chosen plan.
