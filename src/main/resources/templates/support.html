Frequently Asked Questions

How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days. Express delivery (available for certain pin codes) takes 1–2 days. Elite subscription members enjoy priority delivery with no extra charge.
What is your return & refund policy?
We accept returns within 7 days of delivery for sealed, unused products. Refunds are processed within 3–5 business days. For damaged or incorrect items, we offer a full refund or replacement.
Are your products certified organic?
Yes! All our organic-labelled products are certified by FSSAI. We source directly from farmers and maintain strict quality standards. Each product batch goes through rigorous quality testing.
How do I manage my subscription?
You can upgrade, downgrade, pause, or cancel your subscription anytime from the Subscriptions page in your account. Cancellations take effect at the end of your current billing period with no extra charges.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), net banking, and EMI options. All transactions are secured with 256-bit SSL encryption.
How do loyalty points work?
You earn 1 point for every ₹10 spent. Subscription members earn up to 3× points. Redeem 100 points = ₹10 discount on your next order. Points never expire as long as your account is active.
